Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef Stroganoff is a classic Russian dish that features tender strips of beef sautéed with onions and mushrooms in a creamy sauce. Served over egg noodles or rice, this hearty meal is perfect for a cozy dinner. The rich flavors and comforting texture make it a family favorite, ideal for both weeknight meals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Protein
    • 1 pound beef sirloin (sliced into thin strips)
  • Vegetables
    • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
    • 1/2 cup chopped onion
    • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • Sauce
    • 3 tablespoons butter
    • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup beef broth
    • 1 cup sour cream
    • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pasta
    • 8 ounces egg noodles

Steps

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat and sauté onions and garlic until translucent.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
  4. Add the beef strips and cook until browned.
  5. Sprinkle flour over the mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Gradually add beef broth, stirring until the sauce thickens.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream and Worcestershire sauce.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve the beef mixture over the cooked egg noodles.
