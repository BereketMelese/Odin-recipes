Vegetable Lasagna

Description

Vegetable Lasagna is a delicious and hearty dish that layers sheets of pasta with a variety of fresh vegetables, ricotta cheese, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese. This colorful and nutritious meal is perfect for vegetarians and can be enjoyed by everyone. Baked until bubbly and golden, it's a comforting dish that makes for great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
    • 9 lasagna noodles
  • Vegetables
    • 1 zucchini (sliced)
    • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
    • 1 cup spinach (fresh or frozen)
    • 1 cup mushrooms (sliced)
    • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • Cheese
    • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Sauce
    • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • Seasoning
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, sauté the chopped onion, bell pepper, and mushrooms until softened.
  4. Add the zucchini and spinach, cooking until the spinach is wilted. Season with salt, pepper, basil, and oregano.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by half of the ricotta cheese, half of the sautéed vegetables, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta, vegetables, and mozzarella.
  8. Top with the remaining noodles, marinara sauce, and the rest of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
